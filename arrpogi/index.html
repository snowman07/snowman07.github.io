About Me I am a try-hard IT enthusiast with a drive for best quality and creativity in mind. I have always been fascinated of how technology has revolutionized the world and our daily lives. Tech Life In order to engage myself into tech industry, I took a degree as a Bachelor of Science in Computer Engineering from Polytechnic University of the Philippines. Right after school, I really wanted to work in the network side of the industry where I handle routers, servers, etc. But things doesn’t happen as much as I want to. No matter how I plan my career, if it’s not for me, I won’t get it. To make the story short, I ended up in the software side of the industry. Personally, this was a tough pathway, but I find it challenging and interesting.

When you stop learning, you stop growing To keep myself relevant in the software space and for professional growth, I decided to leave the workforce and go back to school. I enrolled to a Diploma program in Digital Media and IT from Northern Alberta Institute of Technology where I gained knowledge and skills on the technologies used in frontend and backend software/web development. But learning doesn’t stop on the four corners of the classroom. As a firm believer of continuous learning, I always find time to learn about latest technologies that are available online so my skills won’t get obsolete.
